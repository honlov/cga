State Level Gatka Refresher - CUM Summercamp

Chandigarh Gatka Association (Regd.) Organises State Level Gatka Refresher - CUM Summercamp 2013 at Shri Guru Goind Singh Khalsa School, Sector 35, Chandigarh from 24th-26th May, 2013.
